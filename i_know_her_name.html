I Know Her Name

"I Know Her Name" is a song by Canadian indie pop artist Will Rio from his second studio album, Sit Down, released on November 8, 2024. Known for his heartfelt and sometimes unconventional expressions of admiration, Rio takes a unique turn with this track, dedicating it to none other than Vice President Kamala Harris. With "I Know Her Name," Rio captures his admiration for Harris’s resilience, intelligence, and historic role in American politics, crafting a love song that balances personal affection with respect for her public influence.

Background

In "I Know Her Name," Rio’s lyrics blend the reverence of a fan with the romantic wistfulness of a poet. Inspired by Harris’s charisma and tenacity, he paints her as both a powerful figure and a muse, embodying strength and elegance. The track pays homage to Harris with lines that intertwine political admiration and personal longing, as Rio imagines a world where he could stand by her side. By mixing admiration for her political achievements with a sense of personal affection, Rio uses the song to explore the fine line between public admiration and romantic yearning.

Composition

The song is structured around a warm, melodic beat with a soulful, mid-tempo rhythm, creating an atmosphere that feels both intimate and expansive. Rio’s smooth vocals draw listeners into his daydream, combining gentle verses with a catchy, passionate chorus. The arrangement of the song highlights Rio’s vocal range, with moments of vulnerability and strength reflected through his delivery. The track’s melody swells as it progresses, mirroring the growing emotional connection Rio expresses through his lyrics.
