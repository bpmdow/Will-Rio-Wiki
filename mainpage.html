Will Rio

Will Rio, 2024

Will Rio (born March 23, 2001) is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and music producer. Known for his unique blend of indie pop and EDM, Rio rose to prominence with his debut album Innocent and continues to captivate audiences with his introspective lyrics and controversial themes.

Early Life

Will Rio was born on March 23, 2001, and raised in Ottawa, Canada. He spent much of his early life surrounded by a musically inclined family, with two older brothers who pursued careers as SoundCloud rappers. Rio initially focused on sports, especially basketball, which he played throughout high school. Despite his athletic interests, he was frequently exposed to the world of music production through his brothers, sparking an early fascination with creating music.

Career

Rio began his music career by producing tracks for his brothers. Over time, he developed his own sound, initially experimenting with EDM tracks, which he now humorously refers to as "a phase." In 2023, he released his debut EP, Crying in da Club, showcasing his early EDM work. Later that year, Rio made waves with the release of Innocent, a single that gained significant attention for its controversial lyrics about Bill Cosby.

His debut album, also titled Innocent, was released on November 3, 2023, and quickly garnered acclaim for its emotionally raw lyrics and Rio's bold themes. Building on the success of his debut, Rio released his second album, Sit Down, on November 8, 2024, a work that further solidified his place in the indie pop landscape. This album introduced fans to a more mature sound and tackled deeply personal and sometimes controversial subjects, including the experience that inspired his hit single Tension.

Musical Style and Influences

Rio's music blends indie pop with EDM elements, marked by a combination of ethereal soundscapes and introspective lyrics. His unique approach to lyricism often dives into topics that many artists avoid, giving his work a distinctive edge. Known for his vulnerability, Rio’s songs address a range of emotions from humor to melancholy, often drawing on his personal experiences.

Controversies

Will Rio attracted widespread attention with the release of his single Innocent, a track that includes lines defending Bill Cosby, a theme that sparked debate among fans and critics. This controversy didn’t prevent his debut album from achieving success, and Rio continued to explore provocative themes in his follow-up album, Sit Down, which, despite its contentious lyrics, gained popularity and critical acclaim. Through his music, Rio embraces controversy as a means of engaging listeners and challenging societal taboos.
